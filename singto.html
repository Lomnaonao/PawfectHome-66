Breed: Pomeranian

Age: 1 year

Gender: Male

About Singto

Before arriving at our adoption center, Singto had a rough start in life. He was found alone in a park, scared and hungry, with no one to care for him. Despite these hardships, Singto’s playful and joyful spirit never faded. He would wag his tail and approach anyone with love and curiosity. Now, Singto dreams of a forever home where he can share endless kisses, play to his heart’s content, and bring happiness to a loving family every day.
